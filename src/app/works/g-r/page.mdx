  ## graphite reproductions
  <span>2017–</span>
</div>

<hr />

A collection of reproductions of paintings from the Rococo, Baroque, Realism and Romantic periods, done in graphite.

In chronological appearance:

- 2021: Allegory of Peace and War, 1776 by Pompeo Batoni.
- 2021: Still Life with Flowers, 1690–1720 by Rachel Ruysch.
- 2020: Mercy Otis Warren, 1763 by John Singleton Copley.
- 2017: Louise Queen of Belgians, 1841 by Franz Xaver Winterhalter.
